In order to activate the mod panel in game you need to press the following.
F10

Once pressed Icons will appear on the screen
If you wish to make a new Icon Press the following
NEW BUTTON

Name the button
in the box that says magic word, do ~commandname
Once done press the Make Button at the bottom middle of the screen to create the button

To move a button keep hold of right click and drag it to its place.

To delete a button click the middle mouse scroll button

To use a button use the normal left click.
